Culinary Journey

A Three-Course Journey

❧

Entrée

  • Velouté de Champignons Sauvages (v)

    Wild forest mushroom velouté with truffle cream, chervil oil and a dusting of porcini

  • Terrine de Foie Gras Maison

    Country-style duck liver terrine with Sauternes jelly, brioche toasts and cornichons

  • Tartare de Saumon Fumé (v option available)

    Cold-smoked salmon tartare with cucumber, dill crème fraîche, salmon roe and rye crispbread

Plat Principal

  • Filet de Bœuf Wellington

    Beef tenderloin encased in mushroom duxelles and golden pastry, with Périgueux sauce and haricots verts

  • Selle d'Agneau Rôtie

    Rack of lamb roasted with herbed crust, pommes dauphinoise, roasted garlic and rosemary jus

  • Risotto aux Cèpes et Parmesan (v)

    Hand-stirred porcini risotto with aged Parmigiano-Reggiano, fresh thyme and a drizzle of white truffle oil

Dessert

  • Millefeuille Vanille Bourbon

    Crisp caramelised puff pastry layered with Bourbon vanilla crème pâtissière and fresh berries

  • Fondant au Chocolat Noir (v)

    Bittersweet 72% cacao fondant with salted caramel heart, accompanied by crème glacée à la fleur de sel

  • Assiette de Fromages Affinés

    A curated selection of aged French and Nordic cheeses with honeycomb, walnuts and dried muscatel grapes

Dietary requirements & allergies: please inform us at the time of booking  ·  All menus subject to change based on seasonal availability

The Cellar

An Exceptional Cellar

  • Krug Grande Cuvée

  • Billecart-Salmon Blanc de Blancs
    Champagne, France  ·  2015

White Wines

  • Domaine Leflaive Puligny-Montrachet
    Burgundy, France  ·  2020

    Exquisite Chardonnay, fresh acacia, almond cream, and a long mineral finish redolent of Côte de Beaune limestone

  • Château Carbonnieux Blanc
    Pessac-Léognan, France  ·  2019

    Sauvignon Blanc and Sémillon in harmony, grapefruit, wax, smoked stone and a vivid, precise finish

  • Trimbach Clos Sainte Hune Riesling
    Alsace, France  ·  2018

    Legendary Riesling of uncompromising dryness, petrol, slate, lemon zest and infinite length

  • Gaia Estate Assyrtiko Wild Ferment
    Santorini, Greece  ·  2021

    Volcanic salinity, preserved lemon, flint and a steely backbone that speaks of its ancient terroir

Burgundy & Pinot Noir

  • Gevrey-Chambertin 1er Cru Clos St-Jacques, Rousseau
    Burgundy, France  ·  2017

  • Volnay 1er Cru Clos des Chênes
    Burgundy, France  ·  2019

Schedule

The Evening Programme

❧
  1. 18:30

    Arrival & Champagne Reception

    Guests are welcomed in the candlelit foyer with a glass of Krug Grande Cuvée and curated amuse-bouches prepared by the estate chef

  2. 19:15

    The Opening Toast

    A few words from our host and benefactor, followed by the ceremonial uncorking of the evening's first grand wine. A moment of shared reverence

  3. 19:30

    Dinner Is Served

    Guests are invited to take their seats for the three-course dinner, each course paired with a selection from our distinguished cellar

  4. 21:00

    The Wine Auction & Awards

    A curated live auction of rare verticals, followed by the presentation of the La Baronessa Excellence Awards to those advancing the culture of fine wine

  5. 22:00

    Guest Address & Masterclass

    Our distinguished guest sommelier presents a guided tasting of three exceptional vintages, illuminating the terroir and philosophy behind each estate

  6. 23:00

    Music, Digestifs & Conversation

    The salon opens for cognac, aged Armagnac and late-harvest dessert wines accompanied by live chamber music. The evening lingering as it should
